Our Amenities

At Mountain Oasis Bed & Breakfast, we provide a variety of amenities designed to ensure your stay is comfortable and enjoyable. Here are some of the features you can expect:

  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the property
  • Access to our cozy lounge area with a fireplace
  • Private lake with fishing and swimming (seasonal)
  • Outdoor firepit for evening relaxation
  • Game room with pool and air hockey tables
  • Complimentary coffee, tea, and snacks

For breakfast, please check our Breakfast Menu for a selection of delicious options served daily.
