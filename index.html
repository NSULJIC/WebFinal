Welcome to Mountain Oasis Bed & Breakfast – Your Tranquil Retreat in the Heart of Nature

Nestled near charming local tourist towns, Mountain Oasis is the perfect escape for those seeking rest, relaxation, and adventure in a serene, rustic setting. Whether you're here to unwind in nature, enjoy local hiking trails, or explore the quaint nearby villages, our cozy B&B offers a peaceful haven for guests looking to disconnect and recharge.

A Cozy, Rustic Retreat with Outdoor Adventure

Our inviting lodge, designed with a rustic yet comfortable outdoor feel, provides a peaceful environment where you can relax and enjoy the beauty of the surrounding land. In the summer, take a dip in the private lake right behind the property, or gather around the firepit for cozy nights of s’mores and hot chocolate. The expansive yard offers plenty of space for lawn games, while the game room provides an opportunity to challenge friends to a friendly match of pool or air hockey.

In winter, unwind after a day of skiing at the nearby slopes, or simply enjoy the warmth of the firepit, hot drinks, and good conversation. While most guests come for a short stay of relaxation, the nearby towns offer plenty of shopping, spa packages, and local dining to round out your experience.

Comfortable Rooms and Relaxed Atmosphere

Mountain Oasis features six unique rooms, each designed for comfort and tranquility. Rooms range from cozy queen bedrooms to the spacious family suite, perfect for those traveling with loved ones. Our handicap-accessible room on the main floor ensures all guests can enjoy their stay with ease. Each room is equipped with modern amenities, including Wi-Fi, TVs, and secure safes, while maintaining the rustic charm that defines our B&B.

Whether you’re staying in one of our queen rooms or the Jack-and-Jill family suite, you’ll find our accommodations perfect for a peaceful retreat. Housekeeping is performed before your arrival and after your departure, ensuring a clean and comfortable stay without intruding on your downtime. Laundry services are available upon request.

Adult-Centered Relaxation

Our B&B is primarily geared toward adult guests seeking a relaxing getaway. While we adore pets, we currently only allow service animals, but this may change as our business grows. We aim to create a peaceful atmosphere for couples, friends, or solo travelers who wish to enjoy a serene, nature-filled experience.

Unwind and Explore

In addition to the firepit and cozy common areas, guests can take advantage of nearby outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and exploring the charming local towns. Each evening, unwind with a cocktail by the fire and enjoy the peaceful ambiance of the mountain air. For those looking to explore, local towns offer shopping, dining, and luxurious spa packages to help you recharge and relax.

Book Your Stay

Ready for your mountain escape? Visit our reservation page to secure your room at Mountain Oasis Bed & Breakfast. We look forward to welcoming you to our little slice of paradise.

Note: Prices range from $269–$300 per night, depending on the room and number of guests. We offer a truly unique experience that’s perfect for a quiet retreat or an active adventure.
