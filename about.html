About Us

At Mountain Oasis Bed & Breakfast, we’re a family-owned retreat, dedicated to offering guests a peaceful and memorable escape in the heart of nature. My name is Mr. Doppelgänger, and at 47, I bring decades of experience to the world of hospitality. I moved to the United States as a child with my parents, who opened our very first Bed and Breakfast in 1980. Over the years, we've built a small but passionate collection of B&Bs, each offering the same warm, welcoming atmosphere that has become the hallmark of our family.

Along with my wonderful wife Frances, our two daughters, Faith and Emma, and a team of dedicated staff, I’m proud to help create a relaxing and tranquil environment for guests at Mountain Oasis. We also operate three other Bed and Breakfasts in small towns across Ohio and Pennsylvania, including locations in Erie, PA, Conneaut Lake, OH, and Put-in-Bay, OH. Each of our B&Bs reflects the same commitment to comfort, relaxation, and outdoor beauty, designed to help you unwind and enjoy life at a slower pace.

At Mountain Oasis, we’re excited to share our love for nature, our cozy space, and our family’s hospitality with you. Whether you're here for a quiet retreat or a base to explore nearby towns, we’ll make sure your stay feels like home.
